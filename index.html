Jens Petersen

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the German Cancer Research Center in Heidelberg and previously the Heidelberg University Hospital (and also affiliated with the Faculty of Physics & Astronomy at Heidelberg University). My research focuses on generative models for time series, more specifically the representation of function spaces on continuous domains (see my thesis), and their application to data-based modeling of tumor growth. I'm one of the two organizers of heidelberg.ai, scientific coordinator for the HIDSS4Health graduate school and a lead developer of the trixi machine learning framework. I was a PC member for AAAI 2021 and I have reviewed for numerous other venues, including MICCAI, TMI, IJCARS, JMI and Scientific Reports.
